About Me ()

Hello, my name is Ethan Petree and I am 19 years old. I am a student at Neumont University. On week days, I get up at 6:30 for my 8:00 AM class, and have 2 eggs and a glass of milk. I enjoy my bike ride to school every morning, it is good exercise.

After I am done attending my classes for the day I ride my bike back to my apartment to unwind and get a couple assignments done. At about 7 PM I go to the gym with my roommate for about 30 minutes. I end off the day with some piano practice and light reading. At about 9:45 PM I brush my teeth, put in my retainer, and wash my face, preparing for 10:00 PM, when I turn in for the night.
