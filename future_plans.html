My Plan

“I pay no attention whatever to anybody's praise or blame. I simply follow my own feelings.”

- Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

The number one thing that I imagine most people will do is try to get a job after they graduate, and I am no different. I will want to immediately start working on paying off my loans as i've seen just how bad debt accrual is. I first need to save up enough to have enough money to last about 2 months, think of it like an emergency fund. After that I will be able to work on my debt and get rid of it as fast as possible.

Sometime during that period I will either have to borrow or buy a used car, probably from my parents to get around. I need to find a decent place to live, which will depend if and where I get a job. My priority will be my mental health over my workload and maybe I'll get a chance to start a family or pursue one of my hobbies a lot more.
